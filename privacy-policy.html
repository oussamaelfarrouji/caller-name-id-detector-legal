Privacy Policy

Caller Name ID Detector (iOS)

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Caller Name ID Detector (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides caller identification and number lookup functionality. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it may be shared, and the choices you have.

If you have questions or want to request deletion of your data, contact us at support@callernamedetector.app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: phone number and (optionally) name you provide during sign-in.
  • Lookup activity: numbers searched, timestamps, and basic usage events (used for limits, fraud prevention, and app reliability).
  • Device & diagnostics: device model, iOS version, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Push notification tokens: device token needed to deliver notifications (if enabled).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as health, precise location, or government IDs).

How We Use Information

  • Provide caller lookup and results inside the app
  • Enforce free usage limits and subscription entitlements
  • Send app notifications you opted into (e.g., “a number was searched”)
  • Prevent abuse, spam, automated scraping, and fraud
  • Maintain security and improve stability/performance

Advertising & Tracking

The app may display advertisements. Ads may remain visible unless you have an active subscription plan that removes or reduces ads (if offered in the app).

If an ad provider uses tracking technologies, iOS may ask for permission (App Tracking Transparency). If you decline, tracking for ads will be limited.

Subscriptions & Payments (Apple)

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple may share limited purchase information with us (for example, to validate your subscription status).

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled in your Apple ID subscription settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate the app, for example: Firebase (backend / analytics), OneSignal (push notifications), and ad networks (advertising).

These providers may process information on our behalf. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies.

Sharing of Information

We may share information in the following cases:

  • Service providers: vendors that help us run the app (hosting, analytics, notifications, ads).
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Safety & security: to investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Some logs (like security or fraud signals) may be retained for longer periods to protect the service.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Notifications: you can enable/disable push notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Account/data deletion: request deletion by emailing us.
  • Subscriptions: manage/cancel via Apple ID subscription settings.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect your information consistent with this policy and applicable law.
