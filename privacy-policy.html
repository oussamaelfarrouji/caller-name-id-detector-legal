Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Caller Name ID Detector ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Used for user identification and caller lookup.
  • User Name: Provided by the user during registration.
  • Search Activity: Stored to improve app functionality and prevent abuse.
  • Device Information: Non-personal data such as device type and iOS version.

How We Use Information

  • Provide caller identification services
  • Enable subscription features
  • Send push notifications
  • Improve app performance and security

Advertising

The app may display advertisements. Ads remain visible unless the user purchases an active subscription.

Subscriptions

Paid subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled by the user via Apple ID settings.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Firebase, Apple In-App Purchases, and OneSignal for notifications. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

User Rights

Users may request data deletion by contacting us.
